Forms & Procedures

Lost Cards

OMNY Card:

  • Fill Lost MetroCard Form
  • Return to room 236
  • Pickup new card in room 225 within 3 business days

ID Card:

  • Pick up form in room 209
  • Pay $3

Absence/Lateness Procedures

  • Use Excused Absence/Late Excuse Form
  • Pick up from rooms 203, 209, 236
  • Fill out in pen
  • Get teacher signatures
  • For PE, write "PE" or "physical education"
  • Email teachers about absences/late arrivals

Early Excuse Form

  • Fill out Part I yourself
  • Have parent fill Early Excuse Consent
  • Get teacher signatures for missed classes
  • Submit to attendance office (room 203) before leaving
